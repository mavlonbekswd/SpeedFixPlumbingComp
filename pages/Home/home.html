Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our plumbing services and solutions

What emergency plumbing services do you offer?

We offer 24/7 emergency plumbing services including burst pipes, blocked drains, leaking taps, hot water repairs, and gas leaks. Our team arrives within 35 minutes in all service areas.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

No, we don't charge a call-out fee for standard service calls during business hours. For emergency after-hours services, a small call-out fee may apply.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

Yes, all our plumbers are fully licensed, insured, and undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest plumbing technologies and safety standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and bank transfers. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger plumbing projects.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all plumbing services. Our quotes are transparent with no hidden costs.

What areas do you service?

We service Cambridge, Slough, Reading, Guildford, Stevenage, Peterborough, and Ipswich. Check our service area map for detailed coverage information.
